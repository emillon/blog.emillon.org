Library

OCaml’s standard library takes a minimal approach : only what’s necessary to bootstrap the compiler is included. So, as a programmer, one often has to write library functions. Several “standard library replacements” have emerged, most notably Extlib, Batteries Included and Janestreet’s Core. But despite their quality, they are not part of the official distribution.
